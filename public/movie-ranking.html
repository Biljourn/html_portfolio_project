The Best Movies According to Villamor

My top 3 movies of all time!

Apocalypto

A cool movie displaying bravery for survival against the oppressor.

The Revenant

Great movie about someone who has returned after being mauled by a bear and left for dead by members of his own hunting team.

Armageddon

Really a great sci-fi movie, a cool manifestation of bravery and heroism.
